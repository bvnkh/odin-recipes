Meatballsub Recipe

A delicious meatball sub recipe, it will leave you satiated and satisfied!

Ingredients

  • A good, crunchy baguette
  • Meatballs, any type
  • Cheese, any cheese
  • Tomatoe sauce, not store bought
Steps
  1. Grab everything you need from the store
  2. Toast the baguette ever so lightly
  3. Combine the meatballs and the sauce in a pot and let it simmer
  4. Transfer contents of pot onto the baguette with a ladle
  5. Eat.
