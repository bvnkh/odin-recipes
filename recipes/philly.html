Philly Cheese Sub Recipe

A delicious philly sub recipe, it will leave you satiated and satisfied!

Ingredients

  • A good, crunchy baguette
  • Meat, any type but preferably beef
  • Cheese, any cheese
  • Onions
Steps
  1. Grab everything you need from the store
  2. Toast the baguette ever so lightly
  3. Cook the meat, and then put cheese on it so it melts together. Let it simmer.
  4. Transfer contents onto the baguette
  5. Eat.
